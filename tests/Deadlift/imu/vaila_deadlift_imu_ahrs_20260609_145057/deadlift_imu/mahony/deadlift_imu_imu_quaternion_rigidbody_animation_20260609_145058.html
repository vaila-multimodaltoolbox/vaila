Quaternion rigid-body animation: real-world frame + translated/rotated cube

Real-world Cartesian axes are fixed (Xe, Ye, Ze). The cube center translates by the AHRS/ZUPT vertical displacement and the cube/body axes (Xb, Yb, Zb) rotate by q(t). The black vector is the unit quaternion axis n(q); rotation, latitude, longitude, and translation are shown in physical units.
